Melee

Includes things like swords, axes, clubs, and hammers. Can be used to swing or stap someone. Typically linked to the DEX stat.


Ranged

Includes things that can be fired or thrown. Usually just a gun. Typically linked to the REF stat.


Exotic Weapons

Includes things that don't fit into the above category. Things like dartguns, flamethrowers, or stun batons.
